Bio Spicules

Bio Spicules Microneedling

Bio Spicules are micro-needles derived from freshwater sponges — nature's own collagen-stimulating technology. When massaged into the skin, thousands of microscopic needles create a controlled micro-wound response, stimulating natural collagen and elastin production without any electronic device or needles.

Unlike traditional microneedling, Bio Spicules work cumulatively in the skin over 3–5 days following treatment, continuing to activate your skin's renewal process long after you leave the studio. The result is visibly smoother, plumper, and more luminous skin with improved texture, tone, and firmness.

This treatment is ideal for fine lines, texture irregularities, dull complexions, and clients seeking a natural approach to skin renewal with minimal downtime.

Please Note

A Pre-Peel Skin Assessment & Prep Treatment is required before your first Bio Spicules session. This mandatory entry point ensures safe and effective treatment for your individual skin.
