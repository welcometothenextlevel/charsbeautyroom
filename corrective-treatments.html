Chemical Resurfacing

Pure Peel

Professional peel for acne, congestion, acne-prone and oily skin. Uses a blend of Azelaic Acid, Salicylic Acid and Pyruvic Acid. In the first stage, rapidly penetrating acids exfoliate the outermost skin layers. In the second stage, slow-penetrating acids gently reach deeper layers of the epidermis — delivering the potency of a 20% salicylic acid treatment without stripping the skin barrier.
