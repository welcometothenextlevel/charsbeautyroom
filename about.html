About Char's Beauty Room

A boutique skin and beauty studio built on expertise, honesty, and genuine care.

Welcome

Where Skin Science Meets Personal Care

Char's Beauty Room is a boutique luxury skin and beauty studio nestled in Altona Meadows, Melbourne. We are not a chain, not a franchise, and not a revolving door of rotating therapists. We are one studio. One expert. One person who genuinely cares about your skin.

From your very first appointment, you receive our complete and undivided attention. No rushing between clients. No cookie-cutter treatments. Every session begins with a thorough skin assessment and ends with a personalised plan designed around you, your concerns, and your goals.

This is your private retreat — a calm, considered space where real skin results are built over time, with honesty, clinical knowledge, and care that does not clock off when your appointment ends.

Meet Charmayne

The Face Behind the Studio

I'm Charmayne — the founder, therapist, and the person you will see at every single appointment. With over 20 years in the skin and beauty industry, I have spent my career learning what skin actually needs — not what sells, not what looks good on a menu, but what delivers real, lasting results for real people.

I built Char's Beauty Room because I believe you deserve better than a generic facial from someone who has never seen your skin before. Every client who walks through my door receives my complete attention, my honest professional opinion, and a treatment plan built specifically around them.

My approach is grounded in clinical knowledge and guided by genuine passion. I stay ahead of the latest skin technologies and treatment methods — not to chase trends, but to ensure that what we offer is always at the highest possible standard. If I wouldn't recommend it to someone I love, I will not offer it to you.

Whether you are here for your first professional facial, working through a corrective journey, or maintaining the skin we have built together over years — you are always my priority.

What We Do

Our Pillars

Expert Skin Assessment

Every treatment begins with a thorough skin analysis. We assess your skin's current condition, your lifestyle, your concerns, and your goals — before recommending anything. We never treat without understanding.

Personalised Treatment Plans

No two skins are alike. Your treatment plan is built entirely around you — your specific concerns, your budget, and the realistic outcomes we can achieve together. We do not push products or upsell unnecessarily.

Clinical-Grade Results

We use advanced professional technologies and clinical-grade products that are not available in retail. Our treatments are designed to deliver measurable, visible results — not just a temporary glow that fades by Tuesday.

Korean Beauty Philosophy

Our K-Beauty Approach

At the heart of everything we do is a deep respect for the Korean beauty philosophy — the belief that great skin is a lifelong journey, not a quick fix. K-Beauty taught the world that prevention, consistency, and layered care are the foundation of a truly healthy complexion.

We apply this philosophy in how we build your treatment plans: starting with the fundamentals of skin health, layering treatments progressively, and supporting your in-clinic results with a personalised home care routine that extends and maintains your progress between sessions.

Our approach combines K-Beauty principles with Australian clinical standards — giving you treatments that are both scientifically grounded and deeply restorative. Glass skin is not a myth. It is a result of consistent, expert care.

Our Philosophy in Practice

Skin health is cumulative.

One treatment can change how your skin looks today. A treatment journey changes how your skin behaves for years. We are here for the long game — and we will be honest with you about what that looks like.

We believe skin care should be joyful, not stressful. Empowering, not overwhelming. And always, always personal.
