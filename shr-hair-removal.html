Permanent Hair Reduction

SHR Hair Removal

Virtually pain-free. Permanently effective. Safe for all skin types.

SHR (Super Hair Removal) uses rapid, low-energy pulses combined with In-Motion gliding technology to gently and gradually heat the hair follicle — delivering safe, comfortable, and long-lasting hair reduction. Unlike traditional IPL, SHR works effectively on all skin types including sensitive and darker skin tones. Most clients describe the sensation as a warm, gentle glide — nothing like the sharp snap of older technologies.

New Clients

New clients must book an SHR Smooth Starter consultation ($55) before their first treatment session. This includes a skin assessment, patch test, and introductory treatment.
