Your Skin Journey

Treatment Journeys

Your skin did not change overnight. Your treatment plan should not either.

At Char's Beauty Room, we do not just treat — we guide. A treatment journey is a personalised, progressive skin program designed to deliver real, measurable results over time. Every journey begins with a consultation and skin assessment and evolves based on how your skin responds. Below are our three core journey pathways to help guide your first steps.

The Glow Journey

For first-time clients establishing a healthy, radiant skin baseline.
01 Welcome Reset or Skin Discovery Consultation
02 Essential or Advanced Bespoke Skin Reset
03 Maintenance Bespoke Resets every 4–6 weeks
04 Add LED Light Therapy between sessions for accelerated results
Best For

Dullness, dehydration, general skin health, first professional skincare experience


Begin This Journey

The Corrective Journey

For clients targeting pigmentation, acne, scarring or broken capillaries.
01 SSR Consultation + Test Patch (mandatory — $50)
02 SSR Entry Level treatments (series of 4–6 sessions)
03 Progress to SSR Level 2 or Dermo Peel Expert
04 Maintain with Nano Infusion or LED Light Therapy
Best For

Pigmentation, acne, redness, broken capillaries, melasma, scarring


Begin This Journey

The Rejuvenation Journey

For clients seeking anti-ageing, glass skin texture and lasting luminosity.
01 Microneedling Prep Consultation (mandatory — $200)
02 Nano Infusion with Gold Salmon DNA (series of sessions)
03 Add Bespoke Skin Reset or LED between nano sessions
04 SSR Glow for ongoing maintenance
Best For

Fine lines, skin laxity, texture improvement, dullness, ageing concerns
