Meet Charmayne

I've spent over 20 years learning what your skin actually needs.

I'm Charmayne — the face, the hands, and the heart behind Char's Beauty Room.

I built this studio because I believe skin care should never be one-size-fits-all. Every client who walks through my door receives my complete attention — a thorough skin assessment, an honest conversation about your concerns, and a treatment plan built entirely around you.

Whether you're battling acne, dealing with pigmentation, managing sensitivity, or simply wanting your skin to feel like itself again — I'm here. Not a team of rotating therapists. Not a chain. Just me, my expertise, and 20 years of genuine passion for skin health.

This is your private retreat. No rush. No pressure. Just results you can actually see.
